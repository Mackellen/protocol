Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how PlankFit ("the App") handles user information. The App is developed and published by an individual developer.

1. Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal information is requested
  • No analytics or tracking tools are used
  • No third-party SDKs collect user data

2. Local Data Storage

All exercise-related data (such as plank duration, training settings, and exercise results) is stored locally on the user's device only.

This data is used solely to provide basic functionality within the App and is never uploaded, synced, or shared with any external servers or third parties.

3. Network Usage

The App does not require an internet connection to function and does not transmit any data over the network.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not share any data with third parties.

5. Data Security

Since no personal or sensitive data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data leakage through external systems. All locally stored data remains on the user's device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

7. User Rights

Users may delete all locally stored data at any time by removing the App from their device.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the App's functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected in this document.
